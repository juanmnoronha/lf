What We Do

We have developed a method
that provokes authentic
cooperative relationships.

We achieve this through forging new pathways for collaboration:
Integrated Cooperation, where we

  • hold ourselves and
    each other accountable
    to walking our talk

  • pool resources (funds, expertise,
    team members) to realize OUR
    collective vision

  • co-create and build
    on each other’s expertise
    and experience

We use 1-on-1 guided inquiry, personal reflection and group dialogue to intercept each other’s unconscious, reactive and fearful behaviors and replace them with reality-based responses. Steeped in compassion and respect, our method provides safety, trust and connectedness, removing the ego’s competing predisposition and freeing us to generously work together.

Currently, we find this level of cooperation mostly in critical life/death situations, e.g., disasters, or in military, fire and police departments. Our capacity for responding in such a cohesive way at all, demonstrates that we are programmed to be moved by compassion, and what’s needed is to expand this response to everyday ordinary life.

We have all the answers to address the huge challenges facing our globe. Our task is:
1. To bring together the various disciplines currently focused on these challenges
2. Support them to work together so that they can view each challenge from a 360 degree perspective
3. Help them unlock new levels of human creativity and impact.

EARLY TOOLS

We have begun to develop tools to support becoming more conscious
and facilitate working together.

  • AWARENESS APP

    Guides you to presence/record feelings up to 22x/day and ID unhealthy patterns we already have a website to link this to

  • FeelWe

    Real time emotional geographic mapping about world issues, connects people through feeling we have to create a page for this

  • ADZ cooperation platform

    Focuses distributed communities on actions achieving collective intention we have to create a page for this.

This App (1) Interrupts the chatter of your worried mind. (2) Delivers you to the present moment. (3) Presents new practices that allow you to make choices in line with your highest good.

The app sounds a gentle gong several times a day to remind you to connect to yourself. Then it tracks and records your feelings along with your activities. As you continue to connect with and record your feelings, you will start to discover patterns between your feelings and activities so that you can change unwanted habits and lead a more impactful life.

This tool sheds light, in real time, on how we actually feel and why we feel this way for any topic, in any place on the planet, at any time. FeelWe goes beyond "like" to provide people the means to articulate feelings and thoughts, creating visual stories on a global level.

A “Feel It” button accompanies current social sharing buttons “Like” and “Tweet”, allowing users to share how they feel about any online content on a deeper level. It also connects people with each other to create a new dialogue thereby driving exponential interactions and sharing.

Leap Forward is proud to be cooperating with GSD labs to develop the world's first platform for visualizing integrated cooperation. Using a simple card-driven user interface, cooperators will be able to visualize their shared intentions, agree on key impacts and observe individual and collective progress towards realizing the goal.

Unlike traditional activity trackers and group to-do lists, it uses Agile planning to focus on the most immediately impactful activities. It provides daily visual feedback to enable cooperators to plan, achieve, and adjust their commitments based on both progress and discovery of the best path forward.
